The beautiful sunset
Why does it not last forever?
The majesty of the fall colors,
Why does it not last forever?

A lover's embrace
Why does it not last forever?
A divine euphoria
Why does it not last forever?

Craving makes happiness feel like chasing tails
Acceptance makes happiness last forever
